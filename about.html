Building the Future with Composites

Ghaziabad Polymers Pvt. Ltd. (GPPL) has been a pioneer in the field of Fiberglass Reinforced Plastics (FRP) and engineering solutions since its inception in 1994. What started as a modest facility with a vision for quality has grown into a sprawling 6,000 sq. yard manufacturing hub in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh.

We are an ISO 9001:2015 certified company, committed to delivering excellence in every project. Our expertise spans across designing, manufacturing, and commissioning of complex industrial equipment, including Chemical Storage Tanks, Pollution Control Devices, and Turnkey Plants.
