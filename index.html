Our Journey

30 Years of GPPL - Serving Customers Since 1995 Globally

1995

The Beginning

Established in 1995 with a vision to achieve excellence, GPPL began modestly with 20 employees and a small facility, crafting quality roofing sheets, moulded components, and storage tanks - driven by determination and purpose.

2004

Innovation & Expansion

Within a decade, GPPL evolved into a symbol of innovation and progress ‚Äî expanding its facility to 6,000 sq. yards, strengthening its workforce, and introducing advanced solutions like fume exhaust and chemical handling systems. Every milestone brought us closer to redefining industry standards.

From Vision to Victory - 30 Years Strong!

2016

Global Recognition

By 2016, GPPL had expanded beyond India's borders, earning global recognition. Guided by our founder's vision for a sustainable future, we introduced Effluent and Sewage Treatment Plants, reinforcing our commitment to environmental responsibility and technological excellence.

2025

Powerhouse of Innovation

In 2025, GPPL stands as a powerhouse of innovation and excellence ‚Äî a trusted global name with over 200 skilled professionals and a hundredfold growth in turnover since inception.
