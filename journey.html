Decade of Growth

Within a decade, GPPL transformed into a symbol of innovation and industrial excellence. Our facility expanded to 6,000 sq. yards, our workforce strengthened, and we introduced cutting-edge solutions including fume exhaust systems and advanced chemical handling equipment.

Global Footprint

GPPL crossed borders, earning recognition across continents. From the Middle East to Africa, our solutions began serving international clients. We also pioneered environmental responsibility by introducing state-of-the-art Effluent and Sewage Treatment Plants.
